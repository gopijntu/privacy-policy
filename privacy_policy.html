Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

SecureVault (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Local Data Only: Bank accounts, card numbers, Aadhaar numbers, and policies you save are stored only on your device in an encrypted database. We do not collect or transmit this data to any server.
  • Security Questions: Your security questions and answers are encrypted and stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Information

All sensitive information is stored locally and used only within the app for authentication and account management. We do not sell, share, or transmit your data.

3. Data Security

SecureVault uses SQLCipher encryption and Android security libraries to protect your data.

4. Third-Party Services

SecureVault does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Your Choices

You may uninstall the app at any time. All locally stored data will be deleted from your device upon uninstallation.
