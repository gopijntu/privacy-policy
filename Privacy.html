Privacy Policy for Secure Offline Vault App

Effective Date: 25-Aug-2025

1. Data Collection

Secure Offline Vault ("we" or "the App") does not collect any personal data or transmit it over the internet. All data you store (bank details, card details, passwords) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Data Storage & Security

  • All sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • The app uses an encrypted Room database for local storage.
  • Your password is used securely to protect your data; it is never stored in plain text.

3. Donations

The app may include an optional donation feature. Donations are completely voluntary and are processed via external payment methods (UPI QR code or link). Donations do not affect app functionality.

4. Permissions

  • The app requests storage permission only for local backup/restore of your encrypted data.
  • No other device or network permissions are required.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The effective date will change when updates occur.
